# Customer Management

## Backend Requirements

- Create Customer
    - First name
    - Last name
    - Company
    - Status
    - Job title
    - Gender
    - Age
    - Email address
    - Phone number
    - Industry
    - Annual income
    - Emp count
    - Address of Head quarters
- Update Customer
- Delete Customer
- Search Customer
- GetAll Customer with pagination

## Business Rules

- Email must be unique
- Customer Name is required

## Acceptance Criteria

- Customer can be created successfully
- Duplicate email is rejected

## Permissions

- Admin can delete
- User cannot delete

## Frontend Requirements

- Customer List with pagination
- Customer Create
- Customer Edit
- Customer Details
- Search customer
